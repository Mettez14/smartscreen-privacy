Privacy Policy

This Smartscreen Dashboard is a local-only web app that connects directly to your Gmail account via the official Gmail API. It fetches and displays your messages from the “Smartschool” label, solely for viewing on your Raspberry Pi screen. No email content or credentials are ever sent to any third-party server; all data stays within your local network.

Data Collection

  • We only request the gmail.readonly scope.
  • No emails, attachments, or credentials are stored off the device.

Third-Party Services

This site is served via Netlify (Netlify, Inc.), and uses Open-Meteo and OpenWeatherMap for weather data. Those services have their own privacy policies. Your Gmail data is only accessed by your explicit consent via Google’s OAuth flow.
